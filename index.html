Foundational Research

Diffusion Policy

Introduced diffusion models as a policy representation for robot learning. The dominant action-generation method across manipulation research.

Chi et al., Columbia / Toyota Research Institute, 2023

ALOHA

Low-cost bimanual teleoperation system paired with Action Chunking with Transformers (ACT). Set the template for accessible robot data collection and imitation learning.

Zhao et al., Stanford, 2023

RT-2

First large vision-language model that directly outputs robot actions, demonstrating that web-scale pretraining transfers to physical manipulation.

Google DeepMind, 2023

Open X-Embodiment

Cross-institution dataset and benchmark spanning 22 robot embodiments. The largest open effort to standardize robot learning data.

Google DeepMind et al., 2023

π₀

Physical Intelligence's foundation model for general-purpose robot control. A flow-matching architecture trained across diverse manipulation tasks and embodiments.

Physical Intelligence, 2024

DreamZero

World-model-based planning for embodied agents using learned dream rollouts for decision-making without real-environment interaction.

NVIDIA, 2024

DROID

Large-scale multi-institution dataset of real-world robot manipulation demonstrations, built to enable cross-embodiment policy training.

Khazatsky et al., 2024

Frontier Labs

Generalist

GEN-0

Generalist's first foundation model for general-purpose robots, with detail on their data pipeline and training approach.

GEN-1

The next release in the Generalist model family, scaling capabilities across tasks and embodiments.

Beyond World Models

Generalist's argument for what comes after world-model-based learning for embodied agents.

Physical Intelligence

π-0.5

A milestone release in the π model series, focused on broader cross-task and cross-embodiment generalization.

π-0.7

The next iteration of the π series, extending capability and reasoning across the model.

Skild AI

Learning by Watching

Skild's approach to training a general-purpose robot brain from large-scale video data.

Agibot

Agibot

Large-scale data collection and foundation model development for general-purpose robots. One of the best-funded robotics efforts in China.

Open Source

OpenVLA

An open-source vision-language-action model that has become a common baseline in the field.

LeRobot

Hugging Face's open-source robotics framework. Standardized training pipelines, pretrained policies, and dataset tooling for real-world robot learning.
